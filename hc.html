Frequently Asked Questions

What is an inventory system?

An inventory system is a process used to manage and track goods and materials in a warehouse or store.

How does the inventory system optimize retail management?

It helps streamline operations, reduce costs, and improve customer satisfaction by ensuring products are available when needed.

What features does your system include?

Features include filtering, archiving, sales reports, analytics, and pre-order capabilities.

Can I track old products?

Yes, the system includes a Date Tracker for managing the history of old products.

How do I reset my password?

Only the admin can reset passwords. Please contact them for assistance.

What is the archiving feature?

Archiving allows you to store old items and data to keep your inventory organized.

Is there an analytics feature?

Yes, the analytics feature provides insights into item popularity and sales trends.
